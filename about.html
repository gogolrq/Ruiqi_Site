About Me

Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at **Texas Tech University**. From 2018 to 2020, I broadened my research interests when working with **Dr. Zuofeng Shang** as a postdoctoral fellow at **IUPUI**. I received my Ph.D. from **Binghamton University** in 2018 under the supervision of **Dr. Anton Schick** and **Dr. Zuofeng Shang**. Before coming to the United States, I obtained a dual Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Management from **Sun Yat-sen University** in 2013.
